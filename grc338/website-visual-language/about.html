About

If you're looking for a cozy spot to enjoy a cup of matcha in San Francisco, look no further than the Midori Matcha Cafe. Located in Japantown, our quaint cafe offers a wide variety of matcha drinks and desserts, all made with high-quality, ceremonial-grade matcha. From traditional matcha lattes to unique creations like matcha ice cream floats and matcha parfaits, there's something for everyone here at Midori Matcha Cafe. And if you're feeling peckish, we also serve delicious Japanese snacks like mochi and taiyaki.

The ambiance of Midori Matcha Cafe is just as inviting as its menu. The decor is minimalist yet chic, with touches of Japanese and wooden accents that create a warm and welcoming atmosphere. Whether you're looking to catch up with a friend or get some work done, the cafe's seating options - from tables and chairs to cozy nooks and booths - provide plenty of space to relax and enjoy your matcha treats.

What sets Midori Matcha Cafe apart from other matcha spots in the city is our commitment to quality and authenticity. All of our matcha is sourced directly from Uji, Japan, and we use traditional preparation methods to ensure that each drink is made with care and precision. Whether you're a matcha aficionado or new to the world of green tea, a visit to Midori Matcha Cafe is a must for any San Francisco foodie or visitor.
