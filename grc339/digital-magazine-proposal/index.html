Introduction

mc mod

This is a furniture/history magazine that will explore the history and rise of mid-century modern furniture, why the styles are still popular today, and who the key mid-century modern influencers were/are. The magazine will also present some of the furniture as both art and functional objects for a deeper design history appreciation.
